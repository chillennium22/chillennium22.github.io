Sound Design Contest

We are proud to announce the theme of the annual Chillennium competition. Last year we held an art competition with the prompt being our very own Chillennium-Chan mascot. This year our theme is focused solely on sound design.
Contestants will be judged by a panel of professional sound artists related to the University and our organization.
The winners submission will be played as the hype song for Chillennium in spring 2023
