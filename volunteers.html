By Students, For Students

Chillennium is the largest student-run game jam in the world. The game jam runs for 48 hours straight, with hundreds of student contestants coming in from across the world in addition to industry professionals who act as mentors and judges.
To make it all work, we need your help.


Being a Volunteer

How to Apply

We are happy you're eager to help and with Chillennium coming up this semester we are currently accepting BOTH Event Volunteers and Committee Volunteers. For more information about the specifics of either role, please read the Time Commitment and Responsibilities sections on this page and if you wish to contribute, please fill out the survey below.

Survery Link

Time Commitment

As a Committee Volunteer you will be asked to help with event pre-planning. This includes attending weekly sub team meetings as well as biweekly (twice a month) general meetings.

As an Event Volunteer, you must be able to commit to a minimum of two 4 hour shifts totaling 8 hours of volunteer service to the game jam weekend. We also require that you attend a mandatory training meeting prior to the event.

If you'd like to contribute more time towards the event, let us know! We are always happy to have a helping hand.

Responsibilities

We are only accepting Committee Volunteers until Saturday, Feb. 13th, 2022. As a committee volunteer, we ask that you be respectful and responsive to your team members. As a member you would be a part of one of the following sub teams:

  • Industry Relations
  • Volunteer Recruitment
  • Event Management
  • Social Media
  • Graphic Design
  • TV

We ask that all Event Volunteers please follow the appropriate COVID-19 protocol and wear a mask for the duration of their service. Depending on your preferences and shift times, you may be asked to do the following as an Event Volunteer:

  • Setup
  • Take down
  • Check-in booths
  • Monitoring doors
  • Checking badges
  • Assisting conference mentors
  • Providing general assistance for participants
  • Provide sanitary support and cleaning

Requirements

Event AND Committee Volunteer applicants must be 18 years of age or older and be a current student at Texas A&M University.

Mandatory Meetings

All volunteers must be a part of the discord, this is where more information and instruction will be given out to you regarding the event. To be added to the discord, please take the volunteer survey at the top of this page.

As a Committee Volunteer you will attend a biweekly (twice a month) general meeting in the Chillennium discord. You will also receive information about additional sub team meetings via your team lead. For more information about the sub teams, please read the Responsibilities section of this page.

As an Event Volunteer you must attend a volunteer training meeting that will teach you everything you need to know about your time as a volunteer. These meetings are still TBD, but they are estimated to take place within a month of the event.

Be Punctual

We ask that Committee Volunteers be flexible and communicate with their team leads.

We also ask that Event Volunteers arrive 15 minutes before your shift with a smile and a driven passion for games.

Benefits

Our volunteers are an essential part of Chillennium, and we wouldn't be able to do it without you. To show our thanks and appreciation for all your hard work, we're offering some awesome perks exclusively to our volunteers! These perks include (but are not limited to) free Chillennium 2022 swag, entries into a volunteer merch raffle, one-on-one time with industry professionals, free food, and more!

Free stuff aside, Chillennium is an incredible experience which brings together hundreds of people passionate about building fun, interactive experiences. You will have a part in bringing this organization to life and will be entrusted with the responsibility and autonomy of leading and guiding participants.

Lastly, Chillennium is a non-profit organization, and can sign off on volunteer hours. We are happy to help you achieve your goals when you help us achieve ours!

Further Questions?

We love our volunteers, and we want your help. If you have any more questions, feel free to contact us at chillennium.volunteers@gmail.com. Don't forget to also connect with us on social media!
